About Us

Transbatur is a leading, agile logistics provider with a global network, offering tailored solutions to meet diverse business needs. Our commitment to efficiency and reliability ensures seamless transportation and supply chain management.

We specialize in optimizing supply chains, reducing costs, and delivering goods on time, every time. Our team of experts leverages cutting-edge technology and extensive industry knowledge to provide unparalleled service.

From freight forwarding to warehousing and distribution, we handle every aspect of your logistics with precision and care, allowing you to focus on your core business.

Our Services

Freight Transportation

Efficient and reliable movement of goods across various modes including road, rail, air, and sea. We handle everything from full truckload to less-than-truckload, ensuring your cargo reaches its destination safely and on time. Our extensive network and advanced tracking systems provide complete visibility and control over your shipments.

Global Network

Leverage our extensive global network for seamless international logistics. We offer customs brokerage, compliance management, and cross-border solutions to navigate complex regulations and ensure smooth transit. Our experts are well-versed in international trade laws, minimizing delays and maximizing efficiency.

Warehousing & Distribution

Secure and efficient warehousing solutions tailored to your specific needs. Our facilities offer inventory management, order fulfillment, and distribution services, ensuring your products are stored safely and dispatched promptly. We utilize modern warehousing technologies for optimal space utilization and operational efficiency.
